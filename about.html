Meet Our Expert

Tom

Founder & Real Estate Investment Strategist

Tom brings over two decades of real estate and financial acumen to TBD R.E. Strategies, Incorporated, specializing in loss mitigation, non-performing loans (NPLs), and high-ROI investment strategies. With a track record of acquiring nearly 14,000 non-performing first mortgages and 4,600 properties as a principal, he’s delivered consistent returns in the high 30s—where joint venture partners netted a return in the high 30 percentile on an annual basis continuously—through secured investments, working nationally with industry giants like GE Capital Mortgage Insurance and Verex Assurance Co. Over 25+ years in loss mitigation, Tom has developed a homeowner-first approach, crafting programs that prioritize the mortgagor’s situation while aiding banks. These innovative programs, first proven on Long Island and throughout New York, were adopted nationally by the PMI industry, with Tom spearheading their implementation across all 50 states and Puerto Rico. By putting homeowners first—helping them in any circumstance—he’s not only provided substantial relief but also boosted ROI by avoiding costly delays from legal orders to show cause and bankruptcies, benefiting all parties with a more efficient, profitable process.

A former real estate appraiser, FHA review appraiser, and realtor broker, Tom has led as president of the Long Island Huntington Chapter of the National Association of Realtors and served on the Suffolk County Real Estate Board and Multiple Listing Service board. His hands-on experience—completing subdivisions, resales, and speculative ventures—complements his strategic insight, honed through extensive finance and real estate coursework and as a Long Island Builders Institute, Adelphi University, and Columbia Society of Real Estate Appraisers graduate. Based in Long Island, Tom consults with TBD R.E. Strategies, Incorporated to navigate market downturns, optimize acquisitions, and unlock value in residential and commercial portfolios, blending street-smart practicality with data-driven precision.
